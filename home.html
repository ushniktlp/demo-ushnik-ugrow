    ABOUT US

    U-GROW, an initiative of Ushnik Technologies Pvt. Ltd., specializes in transforming students, graduates, and working professionals into industry-ready talent through guided training, practical projects, and career-focused learning pathways. We don’t teach theory—we teach application, implementation, and innovation.

    Our mission is to enable professional growth for working professionals, career switchers, and aspiring entrepreneurs by making industry-relevant digital skills accessible to non-technical learners and emerging innovators. We support individuals returning from career gaps or transitioning into new domains, empowering every learner with practical, future-ready training to restart, reinvent, and accelerate their professional journey with confidence.

    Project Training

    Project Training – Learn from anywhere with an internet connection. Our instructors conduct live classroom sessions that you can join online in real time. The training includes interactive demonstrations and hands-on lab exercises.

    Weekend Workshops

    Your team can receive training right at your company’s premises. The course content is customized to meet your organization’s specific needs. You choose the date and time, and we handle the rest.

    Classroom Training
